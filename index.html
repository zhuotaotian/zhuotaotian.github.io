Biography

I am an assistant professor in the School of Data Science, Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-SZ). Previously, I had a wonderful time as a postdoctoral researcher in Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPI-INF), working with Prof. Bernt Schiele. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering Department, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Jiaya Jia and Prof. Chi-Wing Fu. Before that, I received the B.E. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in 2017. I am looking for self-motivated PhD (2024 fall) and Research Assistant. If you are interested in working with me, please drop me an email with your resume.

My research interest includes computer vision and deep learning, especially the 3D scene understanding, autonomous driving, efficient representation learning, unified model, and multi-modal learning.
